REDIRECT-AI

AI-Assisted Patient Redirection from Emergency Departments

Project Description

REDIRECT-AI is a Franco-Canadian project aimed at developing an AI-assisted redirection application. The objective is to safely identify patients who can be managed in primary care rather than emergency departments, with an estimated 15-20% reduction in overcrowding.

Objective

Safe AI redirection reducing ED overcrowding by 15-20%

Approach

Mobile/web application with conversational AI

Partners

CHU Quebec, Mila, IVADO, CHU Lille

Impact

Reduction of inappropriate ED consultations
