Ongoing - Multicenter validation

TIAEU-2

Prospective Multicenter Extension of TIAEU

Project Description

TIAEU-2 is a prospective multicenter external validation study of the AI models developed in TIAEU. This extension aims to confirm the performance observed in the pilot study on a larger and more diverse population, including both university and peripheral hospitals.

Design

Prospective multicenter observational external validation study

Population

Several thousand adult patients presenting to the ED

Period

January - February 2026
(2-month enrollment)

Centers

5 participating sites in Hauts-de-France and Île-de-France

Objectives

Primary: Validate AI model performance (FRENCH scale concordance) on an independent prospective multicenter cohort.

Secondary: Generalizability between academic/peripheral centers, performance by patient characteristics, optimal subgroups identification.

Ethics

MR-004 methodology with declaration N° 27829025 to the Health Data Hub.
